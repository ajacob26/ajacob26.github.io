Introduction

The Honu Hut

The Honu Hut shares the beach and surf lifestyle with its customers. It is a retail favorite of Laguna Beach locals and tourists alike. The Honu Hut has a beachfront location, with a picturesque view along the South Coast Highway (470 S Coast Hwy, Laguna Beach, CA 92651). It is committed to providing the leading edge of beach fashion.

Target audience

If you love the beach and sun, then The Honu Hut is for you! You do not have to be a surfer to shop here as the website features a wide variety of beach-lifestyle apparel. There are products for all.

Customers come to The Honu Hut in search of swimwear, beach towels, clothes, sunglasses, and more. The Honu Hut prides itself with having quality customer service and are able to support customer achieve their shopping goals.

Home

The top of the home page will show The Honu Hut logo and navigation bar. Below will be a slide show of various beach aesthetic photos. As the customer scrolls down, they will see products that are bestsellers and clickable images for women, men, and children. These images will link to separate pages with the apparel.

[The first image of the slide show will show a pod of surfers in the turquoise ocean.]

Shop

The shop page will show clickable images for women, men, and children. These images will then navigate away to the specific filter for customers to shop. For example, if you click on women, categories like shirts, pants, etc. will appear in a menu.

[A photo of women sitting on a boat in The Honu Hut apparel will be featured within a grid of images for men and children.]

About

Welcome to The Honu Hut! NorCal native, Annali Jacobs, dreamed of living in sunny Laguna Beach,CA. With the luck of the honu (Hawaiian for sea turtle), Annali opened The Honu Hut in June 2017 where she shares her love for the beach with customers. The Honu Hut is a retail favorite of Laguna Beach locals and tourists alike. Its beachfront location, with a picturesque view along the South Coast Highway, is a prime shopping spot after a morning of surfing or day in the sun. The Honu Hut is committed to providing the leading edge of beach fashion and continues to serve its customers and community!
